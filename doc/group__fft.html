template<class T , int D>
Array<T,D> numcpp::fftshift ( const Array< T, D > &  x)

Perform an fftshift on the array x.

For a 1D array, an fftshifft essentially swaps the first and the second half of the array. In 2D the upper left and the lower right quarter as well as the upper right and lower left quarter are swapped.
